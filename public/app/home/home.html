  • About the hotel
    Welcome to a place where you can have it all. Imagine a hotel where the most important thing is you. A dream location in which adults get their rewards. A location only for people who know what they really want. A place for lovers of life. Discover a new universe where anything can happen. Discover a new world at the hotel Meliá Salinas, located on the privileged island of Lanzarote (Spain). The elements of nature blend on this Canary Island to form one of the world’s unique natural paradises. In these heavenly surroundings, Meliá Salinas offers all facilities and services proper to a Resort of its category. Guests will marvel at the perfect harmony of the design by the famous architect Fernando Higueras with the unspoiled surroundings of spectacular gardens, botanical areas and beaches with the finest white sand.
